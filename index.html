About myself

My name is Emma. I am a Chinese native speaker, who has been teaching and tutoring Chinese for more than one year. I have taught over 30 students and have finished more than 300 hours of classes since the start of my teaching journey. I am experienced in both one-to-one and one-to-more teaching, and is capable of catering different needs to students from any ages and background.

I personally like to learn new things every day during my free time, and meeting people who also love to learn and improve themseleves truly gets me excited. I cannot wait to share all my knowledge in learning Chinese with my new students.

Why am I passionate about teaching Chinese?

(1) Teaching and interacting with people is one of my passions; (2) Enabling people to improve and succeed gives me huge sense of achievement; (3) It is a wonderful experience to meet students from all over the world, and talk about their life and their countries; (4) I enjoy the time taking with students in Chinese.

How is my lesson and my teaching style?

I am patient and friendly in class, and am also attentive to students' questions and requirement. My students include kids, youth, teenagers and adults. Therefore, I customize my teaching approaches and prepare different mateiral for every student of mine.

Great things about my classes

Personalized Material

Different levels of textbooks are provided for students per their requirement and language skills. During the lesson, videos are sometimes played as well to make learning more fun.

Flexible Schedule

Students can pick the day and time they would like to have a lesson. I would always try and arrange my time accordingly to accomodate their schedule. Each lesson can span from 30 minutes to an hour.

Free trail lesson

New students will receive a 25 minute trail lesson to do a level placement and define learning goals. Plans and homework will be layed out for the students to follow after class.

HSK Test Preparation

We don't limit your creativity, which means you order whatever you feel like. You can also choose from our menu containing over 100 delicious meals. It's up to you!
